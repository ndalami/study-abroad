About Us

HK abroad education agency is an educational consultancy and a recruitment partner in partnership with universities in different part of the world. We facilitate the mobility of student application and admission, deliver exceptional quality customer service and are result oriented. HK abroad education agency has a wide range of partnered universities to suit everyone’s preference. We provide the best educational advice for the right choice of courses leading to their career path. We provide visa assistance and offer travel arrangement and accommodation in Hungary. Our team is made up of professional and experienced members who have an in depth knowledge in our partnered country’s education system.
